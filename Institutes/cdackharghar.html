About C-DAC Kharghar

C-DAC Kharghar is one of the official training centers of the Centre for Development of Advanced Computing (C-DAC), a premier research and development organization under the Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology, Government of India. Located in Navi Mumbai, the Kharghar center is part of C-DAC Mumbai, which has a rich legacy dating back to 1985 when it was known as NCST.

The center offers comprehensive Post Graduate Certificate Programmes with a focus on practical training and industry-relevant curriculum. C-DAC Kharghar is equipped with modern infrastructure, experienced faculty from both academia and industry, and excellent placement support through the National Level Common Campus Placement Programme (CCPP).

As an official C-DAC center, Kharghar maintains the highest standards in technical education and research. The institute has been instrumental in training thousands of IT professionals who are now working in leading companies across India and abroad.

Admission Process

Eligibility Criteria:

  • Graduate degree in any discipline with minimum 50% marks (45% for reserved categories)
  • Mathematics/Statistics as a subject at 10+2 or graduation level
  • Valid C-CAT (CDAC Common Admission Test) score

Steps for Admission:

  1. Register and appear for C-CAT examination
  2. Register for centralized counseling on C-DAC website after results
  3. Fill course and center preferences during counseling
  4. Seat allocation based on C-CAT rank and preferences
  5. Pay first installment (₹10,000 + GST) to confirm admission
  6. Pay second installment before course commencement
  7. Report to the center with original documents for verification

About C-DAC Placements

C-DAC operates a National Level Common Campus Placement Programme (CCPP) that brings together students from all C-DAC centers across India. The placement process is centralized and organized across five major regions: Bangalore, Hyderabad, Mumbai, Noida, and Pune.

Over 500 companies participate in the CCPP every batch, providing extensive job opportunities to students. The placement cell at C-DAC Kharghar actively coordinates with the central placement team to ensure maximum placement opportunities for students.
