About IACSD Pune

Institute for Advanced Computing and Software Development (IACSD) is an Authorized Training Centre of C-DAC ACTS, established under the visionary leadership of Dr. D. Y. Patil, Founder President of Dr. D. Y. Patil Pratishthan. The institute is dedicated to nurturing and developing skilled manpower in the field of IT and Electronics through practical-centric courses and quality education.

Located in Akurdi, Pune, within the Dr. D.Y. Patil Educational Complex, IACSD is conveniently situated within walking distance from Akurdi Railway Station, making it easily accessible for students. The institute has built a strong reputation for transforming students from diverse backgrounds, including non-IT graduates, into skilled IT professionals.

IACSD is particularly renowned for its excellent Java training, with faculty members like Madhura Ma'am being highly praised by alumni. The institute maintains a strong focus on practical application, industry-relevant curriculum, and comprehensive placement support, helping students successfully transition into the IT industry.

Admission Process

Eligibility Criteria:

  • Graduate in any discipline with minimum 55% marks
  • For PGCP-AC: Any graduate or postgraduate
  • For PGCP-BDA: Engineering/Science graduates or Mathematics/Statistics postgraduates
  • For PGCP-ITISS: Engineering graduates in IT/CS/Electronics/Electrical
  • Valid C-CAT score

Steps for Admission:

  1. Appear for C-CAT (CDAC Common Admission Test)
  2. Register for centralized counseling on CDAC website
  3. Fill your institute and course preferences (IACSD is a popular choice for ranks 800-2000)
  4. Seat allocation based on C-CAT rank and preferences
  5. Pay the first installment (₹10,000 + GST) to confirm admission
  6. Pay second installment (₹87,750 + GST) before course commencement
  7. Report to the institute with original documents for verification

Required Documents:

  • C-CAT score card
  • Seat allocation letter
  • 10th and 12th mark sheets and certificates
  • Graduation mark sheets and degree certificate
  • Category certificate (if applicable)
  • ID proof (Aadhar/PAN/Passport)
  • Passport-sized photographs
