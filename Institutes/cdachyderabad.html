About CDAC Hyderabad

Known for its focus on emerging technologies and strong industry connections with Hyderabad's booming IT sector. CDAC Hyderabad is conveniently located in HITEC City — the nerve center of Hyderabad's technology industry — giving students direct access to top IT companies and startups.

The campus leverages its strategic location in Gachibowli, which hosts offices of global technology leaders like Microsoft, Amazon, Google, and many more. Students benefit from frequent industry interactions, guest lectures from technology professionals, and live project opportunities with local companies.

CDAC Hyderabad has consistently maintained strong placement records, with graduates securing positions in some of the biggest names in the IT industry. The institute's curriculum is regularly updated to align with industry requirements and emerging technology trends.

Admission Process

Eligibility Criteria:

  • Graduate in any discipline with minimum 50% marks (45% for reserved categories)
  • Mathematics as a subject in 10+2 or graduation level
  • Valid C-CAT score

Steps for Admission:

  1. Appear for C-CAT (CDAC Common Admission Test)
  2. Register for centralized counseling on CDAC website
  3. Fill your institute and course preferences
  4. Seat allocation based on C-CAT rank and preferences
  5. Pay the course fee to confirm admission
  6. Report to the institute with original documents
