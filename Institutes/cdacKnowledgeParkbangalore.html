About C-DAC Knowledge Park Bangalore

C-DAC Knowledge Park, Bangalore (also known as C-DAC Bengaluru) is the second C-DAC centre established in 1989, serving as a premier R&D organization under the Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology, Government of India. Located in Byappanahalli, the institute has been at the forefront of advanced computing research and training for over three decades.

The center is equipped with PARAM Utkarsh Supercomputing facility, contributing towards the National Supercomputer Mission (NSM). C-DAC Bangalore is recognized for its excellence in High Performance Computing, Cyber Security, IoT, Cloud Computing, Hardware Security, and Software Technologies. The institute offers comprehensive PG Diploma courses that combine cutting-edge research with industry-focused training.

C-DAC Bangalore is strategically located near Bangalore Metro (Baiyappanahalli Metro Station) and in close proximity to major IT hubs like HAL, making it an ideal location for students seeking quality education and excellent placement opportunities in India's Silicon Valley.

Admission Process

Eligibility Criteria:

  • Graduate in Engineering/Technology in relevant streams with minimum 50% marks
  • MSc/MS in Computer Science, IT, Electronics with minimum 50% marks
  • MCA/MCM graduates
  • Valid C-CAT (C-DAC Common Admission Test) score

Steps for Admission:

  1. Register and appear for C-CAT exam
  2. Apply for centralized counseling on C-DAC website
  3. Fill center and course preferences
  4. Seat allocation based on C-CAT rank and preferences
  5. Pay course fee to confirm admission
  6. Report to institute with original documents

Common Campus Placement Programme (CCPP)

C-DAC Bangalore participates in the Bangalore CCPP region. More than 400 companies visit C-DAC centers across India annually for campus recruitment. The placement process is conducted over a month with dedicated support from the placement cell.
