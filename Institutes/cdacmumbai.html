About CDAC Mumbai

CDAC Mumbai is one of the most sought-after CDAC centres, strategically located in India's financial and commercial hub. The institute is situated in the Juhu Nagar area of Andheri, Mumbai, offering students unparalleled access to the city's booming IT and startup ecosystem.

Known for its excellent placement record of 92% and strong industry connections with Mumbai's leading technology companies, CDAC Mumbai provides students with the best possible start to their IT careers. The institute offers all major CDAC postgraduate courses with a focus on modern technology stacks.

The institute benefits from its proximity to Mumbai's technology parks and corporate offices, making industrial visits, guest lectures, and internship opportunities easily accessible for students.

Admission Process

Eligibility:

  • Graduate with minimum 50% marks (45% for reserved categories)
  • Mathematics in 10+2 or graduation
  • Valid C-CAT score

Steps:

  1. Appear for C-CAT exam
  2. Register for centralized counseling
  3. Fill institute and course preferences
  4. Seat allocation based on C-CAT rank
  5. Pay course fee to confirm admission
  6. Report with original documents
