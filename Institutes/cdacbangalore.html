About CDAC Bangalore

Located in India's Silicon Valley, Electronics City, CDAC Bangalore offers excellent placement opportunities with top tech companies. Established as one of CDAC's key southern campuses, it benefits from proximity to major IT parks, tech giants, and a vibrant startup ecosystem.

The campus is situated in Electronics City Phase I — one of the largest technology hubs in Asia — giving students direct access to hundreds of IT companies, global MNCs, and cutting-edge startups. Students benefit from the institute's strong industry connections and dedicated placement support.

With over two decades of excellence in IT training, CDAC Bangalore has built a strong reputation for producing industry-ready professionals. The institute's close ties with Bangalore's thriving tech ecosystem ensure students have excellent career prospects upon completing their program.

Admission Process

Eligibility Criteria:

  • Graduate in any discipline with minimum 50% marks (45% for reserved categories)
  • Mathematics as a subject in 10+2 or graduation level
  • Valid C-CAT score

Steps for Admission:

  1. Appear for C-CAT (CDAC Common Admission Test)
  2. Register for centralized counseling on CDAC website
  3. Fill your institute and course preferences
  4. Seat allocation based on C-CAT rank and preferences
  5. Pay the course fee to confirm admission
  6. Report to the institute with original documents
