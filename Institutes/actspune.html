About ACTS Pune

C‑DAC ACTS Pune, located in Panchwati, Pashan, is a premier training institute under the Centre for Development of Advanced Computing (C‑DAC), a scientific society under the Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology, Government of India. Established in 1993, ACTS was created to provide specialized training in advanced computing technologies to students and professionals across India.

It offers a range of postgraduate diploma courses like PG-DAC (Diploma in Advanced Computing), Embedded Systems, and Java Programming, among others. ACTS Pune is also known for offering government-sponsored skill development programs for underprivileged and minority students, helping bridge the digital divide and promote inclusive growth. The institute is renowned for its strong curriculum, practical training, industry relevance, and consistent placement support.

Being part of C‑DAC, ACTS enjoys credibility and trust in the IT industry, making it a top choice for students seeking quality technical education in Pune.

Admission Process

Eligibility Criteria:

  • Graduate in any discipline with minimum 50% marks (45% for reserved categories)
  • Mathematics as a subject in 10+2 or graduation level
  • Valid C-CAT score

Steps for Admission:

  1. Appear for C-CAT (CDAC Common Admission Test)
  2. Register for centralized counseling on CDAC website
  3. Fill your institute and course preferences
  4. Seat allocation based on C-CAT rank and preferences
  5. Pay the course fee to confirm admission
  6. Report to the institute with original documents for verification

Required Documents:

  • C-CAT score card
  • Seat allocation letter
  • 10th and 12th mark sheets and certificates
  • Graduation mark sheets and degree certificate
  • Category certificate (if applicable)
  • ID proof (Aadhar/PAN/Passport)
  • Passport-sized photographs
